# Product Guidelines

## Prose and Communication Style
We use a **Friendly** tone of voice. Our language is warm and accessible, ensuring that even casual players feel comfortable and supported. We avoid overly dense technical jargon unless it is necessary for advanced troubleshooting.

## Branding and Visual Identity
The Gerbarium brand is defined by a **Modern Dark** aesthetic. We prioritize a sleek, high-contrast dark theme that feels consistent with modern desktop applications while maintaining its own unique identity.

## User Experience (UX) Principles
Our design philosophy is centered on three core pillars:
- **Invisible Complexity:** We keep the interface clean by hiding advanced settings and options until the user explicitly requests them.
- **Visual Feedback:** Every user action provides clear, immediate feedback through progress bars, animations, and status indicators.
- **Consistency:** We maintain uniform spacing, typography, and component behavior across the entire application to build user trust and familiarity.

## Content and Design Strategy
We follow a **Direct & Concise** content strategy. We minimize text density and leverage icons and tooltips to provide details without overwhelming the user interface. Our goal is to make the launcher's features self-evident and easy to navigate at a glance.
